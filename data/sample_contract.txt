2. TERM

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for an initial term of twenty-four (24) months ("Initial Term").

3. COMPENSATION

3.1 In consideration of the Services, Client shall pay Provider the following:

   (a) Project Fee: A fixed fee of $500,000.00 USD for the initial development phase, payable as follows:
       - 25% ($125,000) upon execution of this Agreement
       - 25% ($125,000) upon completion of Phase 1 milestones
       - 25% ($125,000) upon completion of Phase 2 milestones
       - 25% ($125,000) upon final acceptance

   (b) Monthly Maintenance Fee: $15,000.00 USD per month, beginning upon go-live of the system

   (c) Hourly Rate for Additional Services: $175.00 USD per hour for any services requested beyond the scope defined in Exhibit A

4.1 Provider shall submit invoices on a monthly basis. All invoices shall be due and payable within thirty (30) days of receipt.
